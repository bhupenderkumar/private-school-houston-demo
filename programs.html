Academics & Enrichment

Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge, inspire, and prepare every student for success.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-K program is designed for children aged 3-5, focusing on social, emotional, and cognitive development. Our experienced teachers create a nurturing environment that fosters curiosity and creativity.

📚

Elementary

In our Elementary program, students in grades K-4 receive a well-rounded education that includes literacy, mathematics, science, and social studies. Our small class sizes ensure personalized attention and support.

📚

Middle/Upper

Our Middle and Upper School programs serve students in grades 5-8, offering a rigorous curriculum that prepares them for high school and beyond. Our students are encouraged to pursue their interests and passions through extracurricular activities and clubs.

💻

STEM

Our STEM program is designed to inspire the next generation of innovators and problem-solvers. Students engage in hands-on experiments, coding exercises, and project-based learning to develop essential skills in science, technology, engineering, and math.

🎨

Arts

At Private School Houston, we believe that arts education is essential to a well-rounded education. Our students in grades K-8 have the opportunity to explore various art forms, including music, drama, dance, and visual arts.

🏋️‍♀️

Athletics

Our Athletics program offers a range of sports and physical activities for students in grades K-8, promoting teamwork, discipline, and physical fitness. Our experienced coaches guide students in developing essential skills and sportsmanship.
