Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge, inspire, and prepare every student for success.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-K program is designed for 3- and 4-year-olds, and focuses on social, emotional, and cognitive development. Our experienced teachers use engaging activities and play-based learning to help our youngest students build a strong foundation for future success.

📚

Elementary

Our Elementary program serves students in grades K-5, and is designed to foster a love of learning and a sense of curiosity. Our teachers use innovative approaches to bring subjects to life, and our curriculum is designed to challenge and inspire our students.

💻

Middle School

Our Middle School program serves students in grades 6-8, and is designed to help our students develop a sense of identity and purpose. Our teachers use project-based learning to help our students develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

🔬

Upper School

Our Upper School program serves students in grades 9-12, and is designed to help our students develop a sense of independence and self-reliance. Our teachers use a range of approaches to help our students succeed, including project-based learning and mentorship programs.

🔍

STEM

Our STEM program is designed to help our students develop a range of skills in science, technology, engineering, and math. Our teachers use hands-on activities and real-world applications to help our students see the relevance and importance of these subjects.

🎭

Arts

Our Arts program is designed to help our students develop a range of skills in music, drama, and visual arts. Our teachers use a range of approaches to help our students express themselves creatively and appreciate the beauty of the arts.

🏆

Athletics

Our Athletics program is designed to help our students develop a range of skills in sports and physical fitness. Our coaches use a range of approaches to help our students succeed, including team-building activities and competitive sports.
