Our Mission & History

Established in 1995, Private School Houston has a rich history of providing a world-class education to the children of Houston. Our school has a strong reputation for academic excellence, and we are proud to have been recognized as one of the top private schools in the city.

At Private School Houston, we believe that learning should be a hands-on, engaging experience. Our teachers use innovative approaches to bring subjects to life, and our curriculum is designed to challenge and inspire our students. We also offer a range of extracurricular activities and clubs to help our students develop new skills and interests.

Our school community is built on a foundation of kindness, empathy, and respect. We encourage our students to take risks, try new things, and develop a growth mindset. We also place a strong emphasis on character development, teaching our students the importance of honesty, integrity, and responsibility.
