Our Mission & History

Founded in 2001, Private School Houston has established itself as a premier private school in the city, delivering an exceptional education that prepares students for success. Our small class sizes and personalized approach ensure that each student receives the attention and support they need to flourish.

At Private School Houston, we believe that learning should be a joyful and engaging experience. Our experienced teachers use innovative methods and technologies to make complex concepts accessible and fun for students. We also offer a range of extracurricular activities that foster creativity, teamwork, and character development.

Our campus is a vibrant community where students can explore their interests, develop new skills, and form lasting friendships. From art and music to athletics and drama, we offer a wide range of activities that help students grow into well-rounded, compassionate, and confident individuals.
