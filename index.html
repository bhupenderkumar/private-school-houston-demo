Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum that challenges, inspires, and prepares every student.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-Kindergarten program provides a nurturing environment for young learners, focusing on social-emotional development, fine motor skills, and early literacy skills.

🏫

Elementary

Our Elementary program builds upon the foundational skills developed in Pre-Kindergarten, emphasizing reading, writing, mathematics, and science. Our experienced teachers create engaging lessons that foster a love of learning.

📚

Middle/Upper

Our Middle and Upper school programs provide a challenging and supportive environment for students to develop their academic, social, and emotional skills. Our experienced faculty guide students through a rigorous curriculum, preparing them for success in high school and beyond.
