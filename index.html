Why Families Choose Us

Private School Houston — The Difference

Discover why families across Houston trust us with their children's education.

👩‍🏫

Expert Faculty

Our faculty are experienced educators with a passion for teaching and a commitment to helping students succeed. We offer a range of professional development opportunities to ensure that our teachers remain up-to-date with the latest research, technologies, and best practices.

📚

Small Class Sizes

Our small class sizes ensure that each student receives the attention and support they need to thrive. Our experienced teachers use a range of instructional methods to engage students and promote academic excellence.

🏠

Safe Campus

Our campus is a safe and welcoming environment where students can learn, grow, and thrive. We take pride in our commitment to providing a secure and supportive community for all students and families.

📊

Curriculum

Our curriculum is designed to challenge and engage students, preparing them for success in high school and beyond. We offer a range of courses and activities that promote academic excellence, creativity, and character development.

🌎

Whole-Child Approach

We believe that education should be a holistic experience that nurtures the whole child, including their academic, social, emotional, and physical needs. Our programs and activities are designed to promote healthy development, self-awareness, and self-expression.

🤝

Community

Our community is a vibrant and supportive network of students, families, and faculty who share a commitment to academic excellence, character development, and community service. We welcome families from diverse backgrounds and cultures, and we strive to create a sense of belonging and connection for all members of our community.

Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum that challenges, inspires, and prepares every student.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-Kindergarten program is designed for children aged 3-5, providing a nurturing environment that fosters social, emotional, and cognitive growth. Our experienced teachers use play-based learning to introduce essential skills and concepts.

📚

Elementary

Our Elementary program serves students from Kindergarten to 4th Grade, offering a balanced curriculum that includes language arts, mathematics, science, and social studies. Our small class sizes and experienced teachers ensure that each student receives the attention and support they need to thrive.

📚

Middle/Upper

Our Middle/Upper program serves students from 5th to 8th Grade, providing a challenging and engaging curriculum that prepares students for high school and beyond. Our experienced teachers use a range of instructional methods to help students develop critical thinking, problem-solving, and communication skills.
